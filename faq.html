Frequently Asked Questions

What is Grandma's Closet?

Grandma's Closet is a vintage clothing store offering unique, retro-inspired fashion items that bring the charm of past eras to modern wardrobes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment options to make your shopping experience as convenient as possible.

How can I track my order?

After your purchase, we’ll send a tracking number to your email. Use it to stay updated on your package’s location and estimated delivery time.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items. Just reach out to our customer support to initiate a return and receive a refund or exchange.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, Grandma's Closet proudly ships worldwide! Please check our shipping policies for estimated delivery times and costs.
